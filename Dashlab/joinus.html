Getting Started (Mandatory)

This page is to introduce interested students to type of projects typically undertaken in DaSH Lab. Please note that you are not required to be proficient in all the domains listed below; expertise in any one is more than sufficient. We are looking for students who are passionate about learning and exploring new avenues in systems.

CLI, Bash and Vim

If you are new to shell scripting and the command line interface (CLI), we recommend that you go through The Missing Semester of Your CS Education.
